About Aatmanirbhar

Making Youth Independent Through Skill-Based Training

The word Aatmanirbhar translates to 'independent' — not depending on another for livelihood or subsistence. This is the sole purpose of our initiative — to generate employment for the youth so that they can lead a respectful and independent life.

Launched on 1st April 2018 as a CSR initiative of AV Global Corporation Pvt. Ltd., we provide free skill-based training to candidates who are job-ready but lack the appropriate skill set for their desired job roles.

CSR Initiative

Backed by AV Global Corporation

Established in 2007, AV Global Corporation Pvt. Ltd. is a leader in the logistics sector and a one-stop solution provider for all logistics services in India. After completing 10 successful years, the company set up its CSR vertical to give back to society.

Understanding the market need gap, AV Global launched Aatmanirbhar to bridge the skills divide and create meaningful employment for India's youth.
